Have you ever wanted to travel the world and see the sights? Maybe you don't have the time or the money to travel to the ends of the earth to see a museum? Well with Armchair Traveller, you can skip the long plane trip, dodge the travel visa minefield, and see the sights, in real time, from the comfort of your living room.

With the help of our Double Robotics teleprescence robot, you can be in control and take a tour of the Louvre, the Smithsonian, the Grand Canyon or the Pyramids of Giza. Our easy to use software allows you full control of the robot and the ability to look around, take photos and video, all from your home or office. Go alone, or take the whole family, the choice is yours.

FAQ

How does the robot get around?
Your teleprescence robot has large wheels driven by a combination of gyroscope and accelerometer feedback to ensure your robot stays standing and stable. Your robot is also accompanied by a multilingual guide to assist the robot opening doors and answer any questions you may have during your tour.
What do I need to use Armchair Traveller?
Armchair Traveller requires an iPad to use. Second, third and fourth generation iPads are all supported, however first generation iPads are not.
